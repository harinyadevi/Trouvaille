norway

Norway is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful places on Earth. It's home to more natural wonders and it has stunning cities, fascinating history and really happy people. Scenic fjords, ancient forests, mountains, glaciers, meadows, picturesque valleys and quaint villages are some of the key highlights of Norway.

Best time:

The best time to visit Norway is during the shoulder seasons, in spring (between May and June), and fall (between September and October), when the weather is amazing and there aren't many tourists. The high season to visit Norway is between mid-June and August.

Things to do:

  • Take a train journey
  • Explore Mount Floyn
  • Visit the Arctic Circle
  • Travel along the Atlantic Ocean Road
  • Tour Oslo
  • Ride the Fjellheisen Cable Car at the base of Mount Storsteinen
  • Tour the Royal Palace
